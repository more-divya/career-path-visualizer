From Curious Kid to Pro — multi-domain guide

Choose a domain below to explore friendly, step-by-step roadmaps for multiple careers. Each job opens a short, actionable learning path a child can follow.

Domain: Technology — careers that build digital products
W
Web Developer
Build websites, apps and portfolios
1. Start with HTML & CSS — create a personal "About me" page.
2. Learn JavaScript for interactivity — build quizzes or small games.
3. Learn a framework (React/Vue) & work with APIs.
4. Build projects and host them (GitHub Pages/Netlify).
5. Prepare a portfolio, apply for internships and freelance jobs.
D
Data Scientist
Discover patterns and stories in data
1. Play with numbers — puzzles, charts and Excel basics.
2. Learn Python basics then Pandas & NumPy.
3. Learn machine learning basics (scikit-learn).
4. Work on small data projects and visualize results.
5. Build a Kaggle or GitHub portfolio and apply for analyst roles.
A
AI Engineer
Create smart systems: chatbots & vision apps
1. Strengthen math & logic — probability and linear algebra basics.
2. Learn Python and machine learning concepts.
3. Explore deep learning basics (tiny neural nets).
4. Build experiments (image recognizer, simple chatbot).
5. Share projects, continue learning advanced models.
S
Cybersecurity Specialist
Protect systems and data from threats
1. Learn safe internet habits and passwords basics.
2. Understand networking and basic Linux commands.
3. Learn Python for scripting and security tools.
4. Practice on safe labs like TryHackMe.
5. Build security projects and pursue certifications.
Domain: Medicine — careers that heal & care
Dr
Doctor (MBBS)
Clinical care & patient diagnosis
1. Focus on biology, chemistry & physics in school.
2. Prepare and clear medical entrance (NEET).
3. Complete MBBS and internship (5.5 years).
4. Choose specialization via MD/MS or practice as a general physician.
5. Keep learning through CME and clinical experience.
N
Nurse
Patient care, vital monitoring & support
1. Develop empathy, communication and basic science understanding.
2. Pursue ANM/GNM or B.Sc Nursing program.
3. Gain clinical experience in hospitals.
4. Specialize (ICU, pediatric, community health) as needed.
5. Continue professional training and certifications.
P
Pharmacist
Medicines expertise & safe drug dispensing
1. Strong chemistry foundation in school.
2. Complete D.Pharm or B.Pharm degree.
3. Learn about drug regulations and pharmacology.
4. Work in hospitals, pharmacies or pharma industry.
5. Pursue higher studies or specialized roles.
Domain: Aviation — careers in the skies
P
Pilot (Commercial)
Fly aircraft — airlines & private charters
1. Strong physics & math basics; good eyesight and health.
2. Join a flying school and complete training hours.
3. Obtain a Commercial Pilot License (CPL) and medical fitness.
4. Gain experience as co-pilot, then upgrade ratings (ATPL).
5. Apply to airlines or private operators.
ATC
Air Traffic Controller
Guide aircraft safely on ground & in air
1. Strong situational awareness & communication skills.
2. Clear relevant entrance exams (AFCAT/National ATC exams).
3. Undergo ATC training and simulation sessions.
4. Work in towers and gain operational experience.
5. Continuous training and certification updates.
AE
Aircraft Engineer
Design, maintain and repair aircraft systems
1. Study physics, math and mechanics in school.
2. Earn an Aeronautical or Mechanical Engineering degree.
3. Gain training in aircraft maintenance (DGCA/EASA standards).
4. Work with airlines, MRO or OEMs for experience.
5. Pursue higher certifications and safety roles.
Domain: Arts & Creativity — design, music & storytelling
G
Graphic Designer
Create visual designs for web & print
1. Practice sketching, typography and color basics.
2. Learn tools: Figma, Adobe XD, or Canva.
3. Build a portfolio of posters, logos and UI screens.
4. Freelance or join design teams; get feedback.
5. Continue learning UX principles and collaboration.
M
Musician
Perform, compose and teach music
1. Learn an instrument or vocal training from basics.
2. Practice regularly and learn music theory.
3. Record small demos and perform locally.
4. Build an online presence and collaborate with others.
5. Pursue concerts, teaching or studio work professionally.
F
Filmmaker / Content Creator
Tell stories with video & editing
1. Learn storytelling basics and shot composition.
2. Practice shooting and editing with simple tools.
3. Create short films or consistent online content.
4. Collaborate on projects and enter local festivals.
5. Build a showreel and pursue film/media roles.
Domain: Government Services — public service & stable careers
I
Civil Services (IAS/IPS)
Policy, administration & public leadership
1. Strong foundation in History, Polity & Current Affairs.
2. Prepare for UPSC prelims & mains with consistent study.
3. Practice answer-writing and interview skills.
4. Clear UPSC stages to enter services and train.
5. Continue learning administration ethics and public policy.
B
Banking Officer (PO)
Bank operations, customer & credit roles
1. Build aptitude & reasoning in school/college.
2. Prepare and appear for IBPS/SBI/Bank exams.
3. Clear mains & interview to join as PO.
4. Training and rotational roles in branches.
5. Grow into specialized banking & finance positions.
R
Railways & PSU Roles
Technical, managerial and clerical government jobs
1. Identify exam & eligibility (RRB, PSC, PSU notifications).
2. Prepare for the specific aptitude/technical test.
3. Attend interviews and medical/training phases.
4. Begin service and learn on the job.
5. Stabilize career and upgrade skills for promotions.
